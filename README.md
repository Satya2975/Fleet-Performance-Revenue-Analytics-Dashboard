# 🚚 Executive Fleet Performance & Revenue Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Executive Fleet Performance & Revenue Analytics Dashboard is a Power BI solution designed to provide executives and fleet managers with a centralized view of operational performance, asset utilization, revenue generation, and profitability.

The dashboard transforms raw fleet operational data into actionable insights, helping management identify revenue opportunities, optimize fleet utilization, reduce operational inefficiencies, and improve profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Fleet management teams often rely on multiple Excel reports and operational systems to monitor performance. This creates challenges in:

* Tracking revenue and profitability
* Monitoring fleet utilization
* Identifying underperforming assets
* Understanding regional performance
* Analyzing lost revenue opportunities
* Making timely business decisions

Management needed a centralized dashboard that could provide a real-time view of fleet performance and financial metrics.

The primary objectives of this project were:

* Build an executive-level fleet analytics dashboard

## 📈 Executive KPIs

The dashboard provides a high-level executive summary through KPI cards:

### Total Revenue

Measures overall revenue generated by fleet operations.

Tracks operational profitability.

### EBITDA Margin %

Measures profit efficiency relative to revenue.

### Total Truck Days

Represents total available fleet capacity.

### Chargeable Days

Measures billable operational days.

### Fleet Utilization %

Indicates how efficiently fleet assets are being used.

### Revenue per Truck Day

Measures average revenue generated per truck day.

Tracks potential revenue not realized.

## 🧮 DAX Measures Used

### Total Revenue

```DAX
Total Revenue =
SUM(Fact_Truck_Day[Revenue])
```

### Total EBITDA

```DAX
Total EBITDA =
SUM(Fact_Truck_Day[EBITDA])
```

### EBITDA Margin %

```DAX
EBITDA Margin % =
DIVIDE(
    [Total EBITDA],
    [Total Revenue]
)
```

### Utilization %

```DAX
Utilization % =
DIVIDE(
    [Chargeable Days],
    [Total Truck Days]
)
```

### Revenue per Truck Day

```DAX
Revenue per Truck Day =
DIVIDE(
    [Total Revenue],
    [Total Truck Days]
)
```
